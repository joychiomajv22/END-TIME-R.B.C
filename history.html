Prophet Dr. Charles Oluwole Omolase

Consultant Ophthalmologist • Scholar • Medical & Spiritual Leader

Biography

Prophet Dr. Charles Oluwole Omolase — commonly cited as C.O. Omolase — is a Nigerian Consultant Ophthalmologist, researcher, and spiritual leader, highly respected for his contributions to medical care, community eye health, and prophetic ministry in Ondo State, Nigeria.

Educational Background & Training

  • MBBS — Medicine Degree from the University of Ilorin (1995).
  • Housemanship (Internship) at Military Hospital Yaba, Lagos (1995–1996).
  • Residency in Ophthalmology at University of Ilorin Teaching Hospital — Completed 2005.
  • Fellow of the National Postgraduate Medical College of Nigeria & West African College of Surgeons.
  • Awarded overall best candidate in the National Postgraduate Medical College Ophthalmology exams — 2005.

Medical Career & Leadership

  • Consultant Ophthalmologist at Federal Medical Centre, Owo — Joined February 2006.
  • Served in clinical, research, and leadership roles since then.
  • Editor of the Nigerian Medical Association (Ondo State) — 2008–2012.
  • Member of: Medical and Dental Consultants Association of Nigeria, Ophthalmological Society of Nigeria.
