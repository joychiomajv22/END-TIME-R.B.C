About Us – End Time Restoration Bible Church

About End Time Restoration Bible Church

A prophetic ministry raised to restore lives, revive destinies, and prepare a people for the times we are in.

Who We Are

End Time Restoration Bible Church (E.T.R) is a prophetic Christian ministry founded in 2015 in Ondo State, Nigeria. The church is committed to restoring broken lives, raising spiritually grounded believers, and revealing God's purpose for individuals and communities.

Our Vision

To restore men and women to their God-ordained purpose and prepare a generation aligned with God’s end-time agenda.

Our Mission

To preach the undiluted Word of God, operate in prophetic accuracy, and disciple believers to live victorious and impactful lives.

What We Believe

  • The Bible as the inspired and authoritative Word of God
  • Salvation through Jesus Christ alone
  • The power of prayer, prophecy, and the Holy Spirit
  • Restoration, healing, and deliverance
  • Living a life of holiness, purpose, and obedience to God

Our Leadership

Prophet Dr. Charles Oluwole Omolase

Prophet Dr. Charles Oluwole Omolase is the founder and lead prophet of End Time Restoration Bible Church. He combines prophetic ministry with wisdom, compassion, and deep teaching of the Word of God.

Through divine calling, spiritual insight, and years of service, he has impacted lives both spiritually and professionally.

Why Join Us

At E.T.R, you will find a loving spiritual family, prophetic direction, sound teaching, and a place to grow in faith and purpose. Everyone is welcome, and every destiny matters.

You Are Welcome Here

Whether you are searching for truth, restoration, or deeper intimacy with God, End Time Restoration Bible Church is a place for you.
